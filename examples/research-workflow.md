# Example: Research Workflow

```text
Use Terminator to research this topic, organize the sources, and create a structured paper draft.
```

Expected orchestration:

1. Identify topic, scope, and output format.
2. Discover available research, file, and document tools.
3. Map source folders and reference material.
4. Plan research phases.
5. Collect and organize relevant information.
6. Generate a structured draft.
7. Verify completeness and source coverage.
8. Return the result and remaining limits.
